Reading the pipeline top-to-bottom: Of 109,029 contacts, ~73% never needed a human — 64.5% resolved by the bot, 8.8% left without a resolution. The remaining ~27% split roughly 14.6% via live chat and 12.1% direct cases (email, phone, other). Of the 15,871 live-chat escalations, a third (32.8%) were customers who immediately chose the case path in Ada and never wanted live chat. Of those who did want live chat, ~6% missed (no agent available), ~34% were resolved fully in the conversation, and ~27% needed email follow-up. Total SF cases created across all paths: 23,499 in Q2.

† June Chat FCR (36.5%) is artificially high — ~478 live-chat cases are still open.
