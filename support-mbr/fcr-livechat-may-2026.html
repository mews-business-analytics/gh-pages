When Things Go Wrong

% Waiting >5 min by Hour (CET)

17:00 (40.4%) and 22:00–23:00 (48% / 33%) are the worst windows. 07:00 is the sweet spot: only 2.0% wait >5 min.

% Unresponsive by Hour (CET)

Unresponsiveness peaks at 17:00 (33.8%) and 23:00 (31.0%). Late-night and EOD sessions have both the longest waits AND the highest ghost-out rates.

Day-of-Week Summary

Wednesday shows the highest wait rate (33.6%, avg 8.7 min) — nearly double other weekdays. Caveat: May had 4 Wednesdays (6th, 13th, 20th, 27th) and this could be driven by 1–2 bad days rather than a structural pattern. Worth checking against June before drawing conclusions. Saturday (32.7%) and Sunday (27.2%) have the highest unresponsiveness, likely because customers reach out outside business hours and move on before an agent connects. Friday (26.5%) also high despite short waits — customers EOD and disconnect quickly.
